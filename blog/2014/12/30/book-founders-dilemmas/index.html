This book was on my TODO for quite a while, as I was in the search for a some answers related to problems founders of startups and individual projects face.

Have you ever had an idea and wanted to collect a team of friends and execute them? If so, you'll know what these problems might be.

The feel of the book is similar to the Millionair's Mind, as the author relates to his broad study and statistical evidence across a lot of startups. The issue is that only small number of these startups are used for the main thread of a discussion.
